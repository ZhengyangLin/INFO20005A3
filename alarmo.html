Description

Make waking up fun with Nintendo Sound Clock: Alarmo™
Introduce a little play to your wake-up routine with Alarmo, the interactive alarm clock from Nintendo! Start the day off right with fun animations, sounds, and characters from your favorite games. Use gestures or simply get out of bed to stop the alarm thanks to the motion sensor.

How it works

Before the alarm

A character from the title you selected waits patiently on screen.

Alarm begins

Gentle sounds unique to the scene you've chosen begin to play.

A visitor arrives

Someone (or something!) from your selected scene appears. Wave your hand or move to quiet the alarm. You might hear coins or other familiar sounds when interacting with Alarmo.

A brief snooze

The visitor leaves, and gentle sounds play again. This repeats if you stay in bed.

Time to get up!

If you remain in bed too long, a more persuasive character will arrive!

You did it!

Once you're out of bed, the alarm stops and a victory fanfare plays. Hoorah!
